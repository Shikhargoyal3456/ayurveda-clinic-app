Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order?
Go to Orders in the bottom menu. Your active orders will show live status.
How do I upload a prescription?
Click Upload Prescription on the home page, take a photo, and medicines will be added to your cart.
When will my order be delivered?
Most orders are delivered within 30 to 45 minutes. Check tracking for the latest arrival time.
How do I cancel my order?
Orders can usually be cancelled within 5 minutes of placing them. Contact us right away for help.
What if I receive the wrong medicine?
Contact us immediately. We will arrange a replacement or refund within 24 hours.
