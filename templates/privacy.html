Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What we collect

Kash AI collects patient health records, doctor account information, and consultation data entered by registered doctors. This data is used solely to provide clinic management services.

How we use your data

Data is used only to power the clinic management features you use. We do not sell, share, or transfer patient data to any third party without explicit consent.

Data storage

All data is stored securely on encrypted servers. Patient data is accessible only to the treating doctor who created the record.

AI features

Symptom data may be sent to Google Gemini AI for generating clinical suggestions. This data is used only for generating the response and is not stored by Google for training purposes under our API agreement.

Your rights

Doctors and patients may request deletion of their data at any time by contacting us. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

Contact

For privacy concerns contact: privacy@kashai.in
