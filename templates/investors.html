One-Page Investor Story

Kash AI is the vertical AI operating system for modern Ayurvedic clinics.

We help clinics digitize patient operations, standardize AI-assisted consultations, add voice workflows, support online payments, and increase repeat care with structured follow-up systems.

Vertical SaaS Embedded AI Sticky workflow Repeat revenue model
Business Model B2B SaaS

Subscription pricing for solo doctors, premium clinics, and larger practice groups with embedded payment activation.

Category Vertical healthtech

Purpose-built for Ayurvedic workflows, not retrofitted from generic EMRs, generic AI tools, or isolated payment systems.

Problem

Ayurvedic clinics still run on fragmented, low-trust workflows

  • Paper-heavy documentation slows doctors down
  • Follow-up patients are lost without structured tracking
  • Generic EMRs do not fit Ayurvedic consultation logic
  • Generic AI tools, payment tools, and messaging tools stay disconnected from clinic workflows

Solution

A full-stack clinic operating system with embedded Ayurvedic AI

  • Patient registration, appointments, case sheets, and outcomes in one system
  • Voice transcription and follow-up tracking to drive repeat visits
  • Samhita-grounded AI support for structured clinical drafts and diet workflows
  • Doctor-in-the-loop model with payments and sharing built into the same workspace

Why Now

AI plus workflow software creates a step-change in clinic quality

  • Clinics increasingly expect digital operations
  • Domain-specific AI is more valuable than generic assistants
  • Ayurveda remains underserved by modern clinic software
  • Workflow software plus AI, subscriptions, and collections creates strong retention and upsell potential

Revenue Strategy

Start with clinic software, expand with premium AI and operational monetization

  • Monthly SaaS subscriptions
  • Premium AI consultation and transcription plans
  • Deployment and onboarding for multi-doctor teams
  • Payment-enabled upgrade conversion through in-product checkout

Pitch Narrative

Use this in calls, decks, and introductions

Kash AI helps Ayurvedic clinics operate like modern health startups. We replace fragmented patient workflows with one system for appointments, case sheets, voice transcription, grounded AI consultation support, follow-ups, outcomes, and digital collections. Clinics save time, look more professional, and capture more repeat care revenue.
