One-Page Investor Story

AyurvedaOS is the vertical AI operating system for Ayurvedic clinics.

We help clinics digitize patient operations, increase repeat care, and standardize AI-assisted consultations grounded in classical Ayurvedic texts.

Vertical SaaS Embedded AI Sticky workflow Repeat revenue model
Business Model B2B SaaS

Subscription pricing for solo doctors, premium clinics, and larger practice groups.

Category Vertical healthtech

Purpose-built for Ayurvedic workflows, not retrofitted from generic EMR templates.

Problem

Ayurvedic clinics still run on fragmented, low-trust workflows

  • Paper-heavy documentation slows doctors down
  • Follow-up patients are lost without structured tracking
  • Generic EMRs do not fit Ayurvedic consultation logic
  • Generic AI tools lack domain grounding and trust

Solution

A full-stack clinic operating system with embedded Ayurvedic AI

  • Patient registration, appointments, and case sheets in one system
  • Follow-up tracking to drive repeat visits
  • Samhita-grounded AI support for structured clinical drafts
  • Doctor-in-the-loop model for safety and trust

Why Now

AI creates a step-change in workflow quality for traditional medicine

  • Clinics increasingly expect digital operations
  • Domain-specific AI is more valuable than generic assistants
  • Ayurveda remains underserved by modern clinic software
  • Workflow software plus AI creates strong retention and upsell potential

Revenue Strategy

Start with clinic software, expand with premium AI and partnerships

  • Monthly SaaS subscriptions
  • Premium AI consultation support plans
  • Deployment and onboarding for multi-doctor teams
  • Potential future patient engagement and retention modules

Pitch Narrative

Use this in calls, decks, and introductions

AyurvedaOS helps Ayurvedic clinics operate like modern health startups. We replace fragmented patient workflows with a single system for appointments, case sheets, follow-ups, and grounded AI consultation support. Clinics save time, look more professional, and capture more repeat care revenue.
